DATA DICTIONARY

Age: Age of customer (integer)
Income: Annual income of customer (float)
Spending_Score: Score assigned based on spending behavior (float)
Membership_Years: Number of years customer is a member (integer)
Purchase_Frequency: Frequency of purchases (float)
Last_Purchase_Amount: Amount spent in last purchase (float)
Customer_Value: Derived feature (Purchase_Frequency × Last_Purchase_Amount)
Age_Group: Categorized age (Young, Adult, Senior)
